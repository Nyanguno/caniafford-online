How the Wedding Affordability Calculator Works

Our wedding affordability calculator uses the recommended 15% rule, which suggests spending no more than 15% of your annual income on your wedding. However, it also considers your actual savings capacity and timeline to give you a realistic budget.

The calculator factors in your current savings, monthly savings capacity, timeline until the wedding, and the importance of maintaining an emergency fund to provide you with a comprehensive wedding budget plan.

💡 The 15% Rule

Financial experts recommend spending no more than 15% of your annual income on your wedding to avoid financial strain.

💰 Emergency Fund First

Always maintain your emergency fund. Your wedding budget should come from dedicated savings, not your safety net.

📅 Timeline Planning

The longer you have to save, the more you can afford. Consider extending your engagement if you want a larger budget.

📊 Budget Allocation

Venue and catering typically account for 50% of wedding costs. Allocate your budget accordingly for the best value.
