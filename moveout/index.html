Move Out Affordability Guidelines

Moving out is a major financial decision that requires careful planning. Our calculator uses the 30% rule - your housing costs (rent + utilities) should not exceed 30% of your gross monthly income.

We also factor in the importance of having an emergency fund covering 3-6 months of expenses before moving out, as well as one-time costs like security deposits and moving expenses.

Remember to account for all living expenses including groceries, transportation, insurance, and entertainment when planning your move to independence.
